Research Areas

Our work spans six domains addressing critical challenges in modern computing and intelligent systems.

🔒

Cybersecurity

Intrusion detection for IoMT, CAN bus, and UAV networks. Deep learning-based anomaly detection, malware classification, phishing URL detection, and LLM jailbreak prevention.

🧠

Artificial Intelligence & Deep Learning

Transformer architectures, ensemble methods, federated learning, and graph neural networks applied to classification, detection, and natural language tasks.

🔍

Explainable AI (XAI)

Interpretable machine learning with SHAP-based diagnostics, attention visualization, and per-class analysis to ensure transparent, trustworthy AI systems.

💬

Natural Language Processing

Urdu–Roman Urdu transliteration, social media bot detection, sentiment analysis, hate speech detection, and LLM safety & jailbreak analysis.

🏥

AI in Healthcare

Multimodal AI for Alzheimer's detection, skin disease classification, and systematic literature reviews on AI-based clinical decision support.

⚡

Interdisciplinary Research

Post-quantum cryptography benchmarking on IoT, smart grid deep learning, energy storage materials, sports analytics, and industry 4.0 applications.

PhishAttn: A Hybrid CNN-BiLSTM-Attention Framework for Semantic and Statistical Phishing URL Detection
Hassan Ahmed, Arooj Fatima, Abdullah Khan, Shahrzad Saremi, Rania Shibl
Under Review International Journal of Machine Learning and Cybernetics, Springer Nature
Phishing attacks impersonate legitimate URLs to steal credentials and financial data. Rule-based detectors fail against rapidly evolving techniques; ML methods rely on handcrafted features. We propose PhishAttn — a hybrid CNN-BiLSTM-Attention model that automatically extracts local and sequential URL features. Evaluated on Kaggle, Mendeley, and PhiUSIIL datasets, the model achieves accuracies of 95.98%, 98.90%, and 99.80% respectively, with an overall accuracy of 98.28% and AUC-ROC of 0.9979.

From Analysis to Defense: Benchmarking LLM Jailbreak Vulnerability and Introducing the JBDM Ensemble Detector
Hassan Ahmed, Abdullah Khan, Hassan Khan
Under Review IET Information Security, Wiley
We benchmark five LLMs against jailbreak prompts and propose JBDM — a three-stage ensemble detector combining a fine-tuned DeBERTa-v3 Transformer with lexical features, XGBoost, and an MLP over hybrid embeddings. JBDM achieves an out-of-fold AUC-ROC of 99.99% and overall accuracy of 99.85%. Deployed as a guard layer, it achieves 100% jailbreak detection on Gemini and ≥90% on Claude and ChatGPT.
LLM SafetyJailbreak DetectionDeBERTaEnsembleXGBoostNLP Security

Securing UAV CAN Bus: A Dual-Schema Deep Learning Approach for Volumetric and Integrity Intrusion Detection
Abdullah Khan, Hassan Ahmed, Maryam Javaid, Hassan Khan
Under Review ICCK Transactions on Advanced Computing and Systems
UAV CAN buses lack encryption and authentication, making them vulnerable to intrusion attacks. We propose a schema-specific IDS that partitions detection into volumetric (DoS, Replay) and integrity (FDI, Evil Twin) subspaces, each served by a dedicated Bi-GRU model with stochastic feature augmentation. The system achieves 97.45% weighted accuracy on volumetric attacks and 99.97% detection rate on integrity attacks, significantly outperforming monolithic classifiers.
